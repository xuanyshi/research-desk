Electronic Governance Controls for AI-Enabled Agentic Large Language Models in Digital Government (Compliance-by-Design and Evidence-Linked Evaluation) AI

Maram Almaghrabi

International Journal of Research and Studies Publishing

Between evidence and experience: a sociocultural analysis of learning and practice in anterior cruciate ligament rehabilitation

Florian Forelli, Adrien Cerrito, Ayrton Moiroux-Sahraoui, Alen Ricardo Aquino Trinidad

BMC Medical Education

该论文通过半结构化访谈9名医疗专业人员，探讨前交叉韧带康复中循证实践的学习与实施过程。采用主题分析法，结合社会文化理论与实施科学框架，发现临床实践受组织、教育、语境因素影响，知识使用呈现经验性、社会协商性特征。结论强调循证实践是情境依赖的社会过程，需关注专业学习文化与组织条件。
